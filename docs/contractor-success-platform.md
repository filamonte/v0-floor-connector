Status: Planned
Doc Type: Vision

This document captures FloorConnector's long-term evolution toward a Specialty
Contractor Success Platform. It is future platform direction only. It does not
describe current implementation, current product scope, or near-term delivery
commitments.

For implemented truth, use [docs/current-state.md](C:/FloorConnector/docs/current-state.md).
For roadmap sequencing, use [docs/Roadmap.md](C:/FloorConnector/docs/Roadmap.md).

## Platform Definition

FloorConnector is evolving toward becoming a Specialty Contractor Success
Platform: a platform that helps specialty contractors improve operational
maturity, communication maturity, technology maturity, sales maturity, field
maturity, financial maturity, and organizational maturity.

The purpose is broader than managing records. The long-term purpose is to help
contractors become more efficient, more profitable, easier to manage, and more
technologically capable while preserving one shared contractor workflow.

## Core Thesis

Most specialty contractors are under-served technologically. Many operate with:

- disconnected software
- weak communication systems
- poor workflow visibility
- limited automation
- inconsistent technology adoption
- fragmented operational processes

FloorConnector's long-term opportunity is not simply software. The opportunity
is helping specialty contractors run better businesses.

The maturity model is future guidance, not current product packaging. It should
help prioritize platform capabilities that move contractors from fragmented,

## Communications Hub Expansion

Communications should become workflow-connected operating infrastructure, not a
detached inbox.

Future communication sources may include:

- Email
- SMS
- Voice
- Website Forms
- Portal Messages
- AI Voice Intake

Target flow:

```text
Email / SMS / Voice / Website Forms / Portal Messages / AI Voice Intake
  -> Communications Hub
  -> Customer / Lead / Project Matching
  -> Classification
  -> Work Item Creation
  -> Project Continuity
```

The long-term concept is Inbox Zero for Contractors: help teams capture,
classify, respond to, and convert inbound communication into record-linked work
without losing customer, project, sales, field, financial, or closeout context.

This must remain canonical-record-first. Communications should attach to
opportunities, customers, projects, estimates, contracts, change orders, jobs,
invoices, payments, daily logs, field notes, work items, or other approved
source records where appropriate.

## Vertical Success Platform

specialty contractors. Technology services are acceptable only when they
strengthen contractor success, workflow continuity, and the canonical
contractor operating graph.

## Architecture Principles

Future technology integrations and services should strengthen the canonical
workflow:

`opportunity -> customer -> project -> estimate -> contract -> change order -> job -> invoice -> payment`

Avoid:

- disconnected service silos
- parallel systems
- duplicate records
- independent operational worlds

Maintain:

- project-centered continuity
- canonical records
- shared workflow chain
- human approval model
- operational command center direction

Provider systems should be adapters or service layers. They must not become the
source of business truth for customers, projects, communications, schedules,
contracts, invoices, payments, field state, or AI memory.
