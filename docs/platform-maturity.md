# Platform Maturity

Status: Active
Doc Type: Current Truth

FloorConnector is best described as a platform operating-system foundation with evolving UX maturity. It is not an early prototype.

Use [docs/current-state.md](C:/FloorConnector/docs/current-state.md) for exact implemented details and [docs/module-status.md](C:/FloorConnector/docs/module-status.md) for a concise module table.

## Maturity Summary

| Area | Maturity | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| Operational core | Active | The canonical intake-to-payment chain exists across real shared records. |
| UI maturity | Active | The top-nav contractor shell, Manager Page rhythm, Quick-Create pattern, and core workspaces are established; polish continues. |
| Automation depth | Foundation | Internal cues, notification foundations, and manual runner/readiness concepts exist; broad autonomous automation is not implemented. |
| Reporting depth | Foundation | `/reports` exists with first read-only summaries, including Sales Tax Summary; broad analytics/report builders remain future. |
| Integration depth | Foundation | Supabase and Stripe payment foundations exist; external e-sign, accounting, tax, calendar, provider sync, and subscription operations remain future. |
| AI depth | Foundation | Deterministic operational cues exist; broad AI chat, AI receptionist, AI execution, or AI-owned workflow state is not implemented. |

## What This Means

The core contractor operating model is real. Remaining work is primarily depth, polish, broader coverage, integration hardening, automation, reporting, and AI assistance around that core.

Known gaps do not mean the product has no operational foundation. They mean the foundation needs deeper production workflows in selected areas.
