# Future Platform Expansion

Status: Planned
Doc Type: Roadmap

This document describes future platform direction. It does not claim these capabilities are implemented unless [docs/current-state.md](C:/FloorConnector/docs/current-state.md) says so.

## Future Expansion Areas

- Communications: broader email/SMS/portal/manual-log coverage, provider-backed delivery, delivery proof, and unified conversation depth.
- Automation: human-confirmed workflow assistance, reminders, duplicate guards, and later controlled automation where safe.
- AI assistance: drafting, summaries, classification, scheduling suggestions, project summaries, public intake assistance, and support/onboarding help over canonical records.
- Marketplace and networked work: invite-based collaboration first; controlled marketplace behavior only after permissions, compliance, and ownership are designed.
- Integrations: external e-sign, accounting, tax, calendar, payment-provider reconciliation, and adjacent contractor systems through adapter boundaries.
- Distributor/manufacturer portals: future materials/spec/product collaboration only where it strengthens canonical projects, systems, catalogs, and closeout evidence.
- Richer reporting: cross-module analytics, financial reports, OSHA/safety reports, AIA/progress billing exports, and management dashboards.
- Marketing and lead capture: contractor-owned websites, tenant-owned domains, landing pages, SEO/service/location pages, public forms, attribution, reviews, galleries, and project proof feeding canonical opportunities.
- Materials ecosystems: purchasing, reservations, stock movement, issue/return, vendor continuity, and job material planning.

## Expansion Boundary

Future expansion must reinforce:

`opportunity -> customer -> project -> estimate -> contract -> change order -> job -> invoice -> payment`

Do not introduce a separate CRM, website database, marketplace truth, AI memory system, provider-owned billing model, or portal copy system.
